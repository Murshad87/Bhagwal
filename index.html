About Bhagwal

Bhagwal is a village in Kharian Tehsil, District Gujrat, Punjab, Pakistan. This project provides open digital data, mapping, and AI integration (MCP) to showcase village landmarks, schools, mosques, and local points of interest.

History & Culture

Bhagwal is known for its rich cultural heritage, including traditional architecture, local crafts, and community festivals. The village has a history of over 200 years, with landmarks such as historic mosques and schools.
